Lauren Phoebe is an alternative pop/rock artist from the Midwest. She resides in her hometown of Columbus, Ohio. Lauren is best known as a freelance ghost lyricist. Nurturing this role for nearly five years, she has cultivated continuing relationships. Additionally, this role gave her creative freshness in her projects.

Influenced by 90s-2000s alternative rock and 80s pop, Lauren's sound fuses alternative pop/rock and indie pop. You'll hear lines of beautifully crafted acoustic guitars. Traced through Laurens's music are haunting lyrics and vocals. The lyrics of her songs express the changes of aging and the hardships of addiction from the people around her.

Guitar in hand since age 11 and a lifelong passion for singing, her artistic expression was fostered by her musically inclined family. Witnessing the passion in other artists' eyes at concerts fueled her musical desires. She remembers calling her father at 15 and telling him she would one day create a masterpiece like 'Bittersweet Symphony' by The Verve. This song touched her deeply; she couldn't deny the growing desire to pursue music in every corner of her life. Lauren invites you to explore her catalog as well as her social media.
